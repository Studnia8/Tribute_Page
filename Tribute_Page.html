Mestre Bimba - Manuel dos Reis Machado

The man who established capoeira regional

Mestre Bimba during playing on berimbau - the main instrument used in capoeira.

Some facts of Mestre Bimba history:

  • 1899 - Born in Salvador, Bahia, Brazil.
  • 1918 - Mestre Bimba created a conception of capoeira regional.
  • 1930 - Mestre Bimba convinced authorities of Brazil to unban the capoeira (which was banned from 1890).
  • 1932 - Mestre Bimba founded 1st capoeira academy located in Salvador.
  • 1942 - Mestre Bimba founded 2nd capoeira academy.
  • 1953 - Mestre Bimba was invited to demonstrate capoeira to president of Brazil.
  • 1974 - Died at age 74 in Goiania, Goias, Brazil.
